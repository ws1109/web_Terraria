Guide

Damage: 10

Max Life: 250

Defense: 15

The Guide is the first NPC a player encounters. He spawns near the player when the world is created. He provides tips mainly aimed at new players and displays all crafting recipes an item shown to him is used in.

∇

Angler

Damage: 10

Max Life: 250

Defense: 15

Angler is an NPC concerned exclusively with Fishing, who appears as a young boy. He doesn't sell anything, but rather assigns quests to players.

∇

Arms Dealer

Damage: 10

Max Life: 250

Defense: 15

Arms Dealer is an NPC vendor that will spawn once when there is an empty house and the player has at least one Bullet or Bullet-firing Gun in their inventory.

∇

Mechanic

Damage: 10

Max Life: 250

Defense: 15

Mechanic is an NPC who sells various mechanisms, as well as Wire Cutters and Wrenches, allowing the player to construct and activate wired devices like traps and critter engines.

∇

Clothier

Damage: 10

Max Life: 250

Defense: 15

Clothier is an NPC vendor who sells mainly vanity items.

∇

Merchant

Damage: 10

Max Life: 250

Defense: 15

Merchant is an NPC vendor that will spawn once when there is an empty house and all players in the game world have more than 50 Silver Coin between them in their inventories.

∇

Demolitionist

Damage: 10

Max Life: 250

Defense: 15

Demolitionist is an NPC vendor that will spawn once when there is an empty house, the player has an explosive in the inventory and the Merchant is present.

Nurse

Damage: 10

Max Life: 250

Defense: 15

Nurse is an NPC that will spawn once when there is an empty house, the player's maximum health is greater than 100 and the Merchant is present.

Dye Trader is an NPC that sells the Dye Vat and other dye-related items. He will move in when there is an empty house and any material used to craft dye is in the player's inventory.
